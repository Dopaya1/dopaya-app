## Executive Summary

Dopaya is a platform that connects supporters with verified social enterprises, enabling individuals to back impactful organizations while earning rewards from sustainable brands. The platform's core mission is "Supporting real impact made rewarding" - creating a transparent system where donations to social enterprises translate into tangible rewards from brands committed to sustainability and positive change.

## How It Works

### For Supporters

1. **Choose a Social Enterprise**: Browse and select from verified social enterprises addressing various global challenges
2. **Make a Contribution**: Support the enterprise financially
3. **Earn Impact Points**: Receive points based on your contribution amount
4. **Redeem Rewards**: Use impact points to unlock exclusive rewards from partner brands
5. **Track Impact**: Monitor your personal impact through a comprehensive dashboard

### Impact Points System

- Points are earned based on donation amounts
- Points can be redeemed for exclusive products and experiences
- Impact ranking system with tiers:
  - **Impact Hero** (starting at 650 points)
  - **Impact Champion** (1000 points) - Premium rewards
  - **Impact Legend** (2000 points) - Exclusive access
